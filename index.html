About

Jessica has served the majority of her career in the healthcare helping other improve their quality of life. She currently holds a Bachelor degree in Health Education and Respiratory Therapy. Her spirit of curiosity and eagerness to learn has driven her to pursue a career in technology. Currently, she is enrolled in a coding bootcamp at Southern Methodist University where she will gain the skills and certification to become a full stack web developer.

She has enjoyed the challenges of front-end web development using JavaScript to build working and interactive games. She will continue to grow her knowledge base by diving into the back-end learning MongoDB, Express, React, and Node js. She aspires to position herself where she can continue to master the skills of web development and build creative and innovate solutions to positively impact lives on a larger scale.
